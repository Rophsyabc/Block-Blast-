# Privacy Policy for Block Blast

**Last Updated:** December 8, 2025

## Introduction

Soardev Games ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") operates the Block Blast mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection when you use our App.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Automatically Collected Information
When you use Block Blast, we automatically collect:
- **Device Information:** Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Analytics:** Game statistics, level progress, scores, gameplay patterns
- **Crash and Error Data:** Technical logs to improve app stability and performance
- **Ad Interaction Data:** Information about how you interact with advertisements

### 1.2 Information You Provide
- **Game Data:** Your in-game progress, coins, theme preferences, and game settings
- **Contact Information:** If you contact us with feedback or support requests

### 1.3 Third-Party Data Collection
We use the following third-party services:
- **Google AdMob:** For displaying advertisements. AdMob collects advertising identifiers and may track user behavior for personalized ads. [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- **Google Play Services:** For app functionality and analytics. [Google Play Privacy](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/10144311)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:
- Deliver and improve the App and user experience
- Display personalized advertisements
- Analyze game metrics and user engagement
- Fix bugs and prevent fraud
- Comply with legal obligations
- Send you service-related announcements (if applicable)

## 3. Data Storage and Security

### 3.1 Storage
- Game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device using SharedPreferences
- We do not store your personal data on external servers
- Data persists even after app uninstallation until manually cleared

### 3.2 Security Measures
- We implement standard security practices to protect data
- Your device's built-in security features protect local data
- We do not encrypt local game data; please keep your device secure

## 4. AdMob and Google Ads

Block Blast uses Google AdMob for advertising:
- **Ad Types:** Banner ads, interstitial ads, rewarded video ads, and app open ads
- **Personalized Ads:** Google may use your data to show personalized advertisements based on your interests
- **Opting Out:** You can opt out of personalized ads in your device's Google settings under "Ads"

For more information on Google's ad practices, visit [Google's Advertising Policies](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads)

## 5. Permissions Explained

Block Blast requests the following permissions:

| Permission | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| **INTERNET** | Required for displaying ads from Google AdMob |
| **ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE** | Allows app to check network connectivity for ads |
| **VIBRATE** | Enables haptic feedback during gameplay |

## 6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Block Blast does not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years old. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete such information and terminate the child's account if applicable.

If you believe a child has provided information, please contact us immediately.

## 7. Data Retention

- **Local Game Data:** Retained on your device indefinitely until you uninstall the app or clear app data
- **Analytics Data:** Retained by Google AdMob and Google Play Services per their retention policies (typically 24 months)
- **No Cloud Sync:** Your game progress is not backed up to cloud services

## 8. Third-Party Links and Services

Block Blast may contain links to third-party services, websites, or advertisements. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party services, and we are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies independently.

## 13. Data Deletion

To delete your game data:
1. Open **Settings** on your device
2. Navigate to **Apps** → **Block Blast**
3. Tap **Storage** → **Clear Data** or **Uninstall**

Uninstalling the app will remove all local game data from your device.
